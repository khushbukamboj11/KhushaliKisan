Khushali Kisan Sewa Story

Khushali Kisan Sewa is an agriculture retail chain that operates an agri-enabled e-commerce platform offering a wide range of agricultural products and services. It was founded with the vision of transforming the farming landscape in India and addressing the challenges faced by farmers. The founder was inspired to start the business after witnessing the struggles of farmers who lacked adequate knowledge and resources to combat the increasing prevalence of crop diseases.

In this era, where various varieties of fertilizers and chemicals are present in the market, it can be challenging for a farmer to choose the correct product, especially when they are illiterate or have only partial knowledge of farming, which can be harmful to both breeders and others too.

Therefore, for bettering cultivators and for the better growth of crops, the organization created an accessible platform where farmers can get consultations, online and offline agrochemical stores, rent types of farming equipment, and warehouses to uproot bewilderment and create a diverse awareness of usage and buying of the correct agro-products with the 30 years of experience of the organization.

Who We Are

Agroganic is a premier Ag-tech start-up created with the mission of ensuring farmers get access to the right inputs at the right time under the brand name of Khushali Kisan Sewa. We provide an agri-enabled E-commerce platform for fertilizers, chemicals, and expert agricultural advisories.

Our cold room solutions help preserve perishable crops and vegetables. With scientific methodologies, composting practices, and soil improvement techniques, we guide farmers toward modern farming approaches.

With our one-in-all platform, farmers get complete solutions both offline and online. We already have 25+ centers and are expanding to hundreds more.

Our Vision

Khushali Kisan has a vision for sustainable and environmental protection. It is our goal to ensure everyone consumes healthy and safe food. With the variety of fertilizers and chemicals available today, choosing the right product can be challenging. Our platform provides consultations, online and offline agrochemical stores, farming equipment, and awareness of correct usage for better crop growth. We aim to build healthy and viable environments for both farmers and consumers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate confusion among farmers through expert guidance about cropping practices, equipment usage, consultancy, and warehouse services. We strive to help farmers create high-quality horticulture and improve their livelihood. Backed by 30 years of experience and deep knowledge of agro-chemicals, we aim to deliver impactful and sustainable outcomes.
