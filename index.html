About Us Khushali Kisan

Agroganic is a leading agri-tech startup with a mission to provide farmers with the right inputs at the right time under the brand name of "Khushali Kisan Sewa." Our-Core Value

Relationships

Our company values the people behind agriculture and aims to facilitate connections between small farmers to promote knowledge sharing, resource pooling, and collective growth through partnerships, volunteerism, and mentorship.

Service

Our company is dedicated to serving the wider community by establishing a trustworthy market for high-quality farm products that benefit both consumers and small-scale farmers, ensuring satisfaction for all.

In a dynamic agricultural landscape, we prioritize continuous education and training to enhance our skills and optimize the use of available resources for all members of the value chain, enabling us to adapt to the challenges of a changing world.

Problems Statement

Lack of agricultural machinery and equipments

The farming process is hampered by lack of farming-related machinery. This cause the crops inferior quality. The majority of farmers cannot afford farm equipment because it is pricey.

Lack of post-harvest storage infrastructure

Inadequate post-harvest crop loss, this cause farming failures especially for smallholder farmers. losses that occur after harvest, the main causes of which are inadequate storage infrastructure.

Dealing with middlemen and traders

Lack of market knowledge leads to reduced profits for farmers when selling their products, and the oversupply of crops at the end of the season further depresses prices, resulting in overexploitation and decreased revenue potential in agriculture.

Lack of capital/credit

The success of agricultural activities depends on essential components such as chemicals, seedlings, and agri-tools, but many Indian farmers lack the resources to invest in them, putting agricultural development at risk.
